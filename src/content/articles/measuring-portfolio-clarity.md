A portfolio should answer the reader's first questions quickly: what does this person build, how do they think, and can I trust the quality?

I review pages by scanning hierarchy, project evidence, and the clarity of outcomes. Decorative choices matter less than whether the work is easy to understand.

The strongest portfolios feel organized before they feel impressive.
